About Us

The NayePankh Foundation is a registered, youth-led non-governmental organization (NGO) based in Uttar Pradesh, India. It focuses on uplifting underprivileged communities by providing education, food distribution, sanitary products, and clothing. It is also known for offering extensive virtual internship programs.

Core Initiatives & Focus Areas:

  • Education & Child Welfare: Supports underprivileged children with school supplies, uniforms, and fundamental educational support.
  • >Hygiene & Women Empowerment: Conducts menstrual hygiene awareness campaigns and distributes sanitary pads.
  • Hunger Relief: Provides food to underprivileged families and stray animals.
